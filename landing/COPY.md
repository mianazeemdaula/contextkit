## 9. FAQ

**Section headline**: `Quick answers`

1. **Is it safe to save my prompts here?**
   Yes. Your prompts are encrypted on our servers and only you can see them. You can delete any prompt, or your whole account, at any time.

2. **Do I have to install anything?**
   You add a small browser extension in about ten seconds. The web app works in any modern browser without a download.

3. **Which AI tools does it work with?**
   ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, Poe, and most chat boxes on the web. If you can type in it, you can paste into it.

4. **If I cancel, do I lose my saved prompts?**
   No. You drop back to the Free plan and keep your first five prompts. The rest stay safe for 90 days in case you come back.

5. **Is the free plan really free?**
   Yes, forever. No card on signup. You only pay if you want more prompts or sync.

6. **How is this different from ChatGPT custom instructions?**
   Custom instructions only work inside ChatGPT, and you get one set. ContextKit gives you many named prompts that work in every AI tool you use.

7. **Can my team share prompts?**
   Yes. The Team plan gives you a shared library, so a new hire can sound on-brand on day one.

8. **Will you train AI on my data?**
   No. We never sell, share, or use your prompts to train any AI model. Ever.

## Designer notes

- **Layout priorities**: Keep the hero above the fold on a 13-inch laptop. Lead with the split-screen demo, then pain agitation, then the 3-step flow. Pricing belongs near the bottom but above FAQ, since this audience reads top-down before committing.
- **Color and mood**: Warm off-white background, soft violet primary, friendly serif for headlines paired with a clean sans for body. Avoid the "developer dark mode" look — these users are not coders and respond to brightness, whitespace, and rounded corners.
- **Must-have visual elements**: Animated hero showing the one-click paste, a real-looking browser popup mock, real-photo testimonial avatars (not illustrations), and the pricing card with a subtle "Most popular" ribbon on Personal.
- **A/B test ideas**: (a) Hero headline "Stop re-explaining yourself to every AI" vs. "Save your AI setup. Paste it anywhere." (b) Free plan saved-prompt cap of 5 vs. 10. (c) Primary CTA "Start free" vs. "Save my first prompt".
- **Accessibility**: Body type at 17px minimum, contrast ratio of 4.5:1 or higher, every icon paired with a text label, demo video captioned, all CTAs keyboard-focusable with a clear focus ring, and no color-only signals (use icons plus color for the "Most popular" badge).
